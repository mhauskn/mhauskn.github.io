About

I build AI agents that learn continually and work reliably.

I'm a Principal AI Researcher at DataRobot. Top of mind right now: continual learning for AI agents — systems that improve on the job, retain what works, and stay dependable as the world around them shifts.

That interest is the latest turn in a fifteen-year thread: how do you train an agent to act capably in an environment it doesn't fully understand? I've chased that question through deep RL agents playing Atari from pixels, text-game agents reading and acting in language worlds, and Pareto-optimal generative AI workflows.

  • nowDataRobot — Principal AI Researcher. Continual learning and reliability for AI agents.
  • 2021–2023Latitude AI — Staff Scientist. Autonomy for self-driving vehicles.
  • 2016–2021Microsoft Research — Researcher. RL, text-game agents, program synthesis.
  • 2010–2016UT Austin — PhD in Computer Science, advised by Peter Stone.
